This project summarizes Mohammad Alkurdi's Bank of Palestine business-side QA/UAT experience in card and payment-system contexts.

Work included PowerCARD-Switch, ATM, POS, mobile POS, online payment gateway, card issuance, instant issuance, transaction routing, reversals, settlement-related outputs, audit trails, and post-deployment validation. The security relevance comes from compliance-aware testing, fraud-sensitive scenarios, data masking checks, limits, blacklisted-card behavior, and audit evidence.
