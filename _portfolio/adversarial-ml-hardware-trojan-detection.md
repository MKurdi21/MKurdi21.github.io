This project summarizes Mohammad Alkurdi's M.S. research and early Ph.D. research under Professor Fathi Amsaad. The work connected adversarial machine learning, hardware Trojan detection, side-channel or ring-oscillator-informed measurements, and robust machine-learning pipelines.

The public description intentionally avoids restricted datasets or unpublished implementation details. The research emphasis is on robustness evaluation and trustworthy AI methods for hardware-security-sensitive settings.
